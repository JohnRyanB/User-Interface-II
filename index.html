Roll For Initiative

Hello, and welcome to my page. This seems like a good place to talk about my passion, Tabletop gaming.

I have been playing tabletop games for half of my life. I love making maps, monsters, NPCs, and miniatures utilizing web and desktop based apps as well as my 3d printer and paint set.

This hobby is my rock. It keeps me grounded when the stress of work or classes gets to me. My dog is even named after a plane of existence from Dungeons and Dragons, Faewild, or Fae for short is just as chaotic as the name implies.
